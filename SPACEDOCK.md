# KSP Launch Countdown

---

## English Version

### Description

**KSP Launch Countdown** is a mod for Kerbal Space Program 1 that provides a professional launch countdown experience. It simulates real-world space launch countdown procedures with countdown voice announcements and automated launch sequence execution.

### Features

- **Professional Countdown Sequence** — Complete launch countdown simulating real space launch procedures
- **12 Voice Presets** — LM-1, LM-2F, Saturn V, Soyuz, Ariane 5, Delta IV, SpaceX, Starship, Space Shuttle, Taiyuan, Wenchang, Xichang
- **Dual Audio Modes** — Single-segment and multi-segment (p1/p2) countdown audio
- **Smart Launch Control** — Auto SAS (3 retries), auto throttle, staging options
- **Volume Control** — In-menu slider (0%~100%) with auto-save
- **Multi-language UI** — Chinese, English, Russian
- **Pre-Launch Safety Checks** — Launch pad status, electric charge, engine state detection
- **Custom Voice Packs** — Add your own countdown audio

### Installation

1. Download and extract the mod package
2. Copy `GameData/KSPLaunchCountdown/` folder to your KSP `GameData/` directory
3. Launch KSP and enter a flight scene
4. Click the toolbar icon or press `Ctrl+L` to open the countdown menu

### Requirements

- **KSP Version**: 1.12.x
- **.NET Framework**: 4.7.2 (built into KSP)
- **No dependencies** — Standalone mod

## 中文版本

### 描述

**KSP Launch Countdown** 是一个为《坎巴拉太空计划》(KSP) 1 开发的模组，提供专业的发射倒计时体验。模拟真实航天发射倒计时流程，包含倒计时语音播报和自动化发射序列执行。

### 功能特性

- **专业倒计时流程** — 完整的发射倒计时序列，模拟真实航天发射程序
- **12个语音包** — 长征一号、长征二号F、土星五号、联盟号、阿丽亚娜5、德尔塔IV、SpaceX、星舰、航天飞机、太原、文昌、西昌
- **双音频模式** — 单段音频和多段音频(p1/p2)两种模式
- **智能发射控制** — 自动开启SAS(最多3次重试)、自动油门、分级选项
- **音量控制** — 菜单内滑块调节(0%~100%)，自动保存
- **多语言界面** — 中文、英文、俄文
- **发射前安全检查** — 发射台状态、电量检测、发动机状态检测
- **自定义语音包** — 支持添加自定义倒计时语音
